Data residency requirements

The AI Gateway supports deployments in the US and the EU, providing data residency guarantees for select providers. For others, data residency is not guaranteed and data may be processed globally. Before committing to data localization in client communications, please consult Firm Legal and CORE/Risk for review and guidance.
